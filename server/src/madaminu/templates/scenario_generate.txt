Generate a murder mystery scenario skeleton for the following players.

## Narrative Structure

The story starts BEFORE the murder. Players gather at a location, introduce themselves, and interact.
Then the murder occurs as a dramatic event. After that, investigation and discussion begin.

Timeline:
1. Storytelling: Host reads the opening narrative aloud (victim is alive)
2. Opening: Victim greets everyone, then each player introduces themselves
3. Murder Event: Dramatic cutscene — victim is found dead
4. Murder Briefing: Details of the crime are revealed, evidence/alibi cards are dealt
5. Investigation cycles: discussion → planning → investigation (repeated N times)
6. Voting: Final discussion and vote

## Map Structure

部屋の一覧のみ出力。廊下・階段・玄関・接続グラフはサーバーが自動生成。

### Areas
- 各エリアは建物のフロアやゾーン（1階、2階、中庭、離れ etc.）
- area_type: "indoor"（建物内）, "outdoor"（屋外）, "semi_outdoor"（テラス等）
- floor_order: フロア順番（0=1階, 1=2階, ...）。屋外は独立した番号

### Rooms
- 調査対象の部屋のみ出力。廊下・階段・玄関は出力しない
- size: 1=小部屋(features 3個), 2=中部屋(features 6個), 3=大部屋(features 9個)
- features: 部屋内の調査ポイント（机の引き出し、本棚、窓際など）

### Guidelines
- 3〜5エリア、調査可能な部屋を最低8個以上
- 各エリアに最低2つの部屋
- 少なくとも1つの施錠可能・立入制限のある部屋

## Character Profile Enhancement

Before generating the scenario, review each player's profile:
- Non-human characters (animal, robot): add plausible communication reason
- Short personality/background: expand to 2-3 sentences
- Empty appearance: generate based on gender, age, occupation

### CRITICAL: Profile Consistency Check

シナリオ内の全テキストは各キャラクターの性別・年齢・職業・経歴と矛盾しないこと:
- 50代男性を「令嬢」と呼ばない
- 20代に「30年前の事件」を経験させない
- 性別に合った呼称（息子/娘、兄/姉）
- キャラクター間の関係が年齢的に成立するか

## self_introduction vs public_info

These are DIFFERENT fields with different purposes:

- **self_introduction**: 一人称のセリフ。声に出して読むもの。
  - 例: 「どうも、白鳥麗子です。東都大学病院で心臓外科を担当しています。恒一さんとは大学時代からの付き合いで、今回のお招きに喜んで参りました。」
  - キャラクターの口調・性格が伝わること
  - 建前の来訪理由を含む
- **public_info**: 三人称のプロフィール。手帳に記載される情報。
  - 例: 「東都大学病院の心臓外科チーフ。被害者の大学時代の同期で、毎年この館に招かれている。佐伯涼子とは同業者として面識がある。」
  - 客観的な事実情報
  - 他キャラクターとの関係を含む
  - Do NOT include: アリバイ、事件への言及、秘密のにおわせ

## Secrets, Objectives, and Relationships

- Each player's secret_info and objective MUST involve relationships with other specific players
- Each player should have at least one ally and one adversary
- The criminal's objective: hiding their crime + personal goal
- Non-criminal PRIMARY objective: identify the criminal
  - However, if the criminal turns out to be a specific person, it causes disadvantage
  - SECONDARY personal objective also required (protect someone, recover item, hide past mistake)

## Initial Evidence & Alibis (distributed at murder briefing)

Each player receives **2 evidence cards** and **3 alibi cards**:

- **initial_evidences** (2 cards):
  - Card 1: 直接的な手がかり（物的証拠、目撃情報）
  - Card 2: 間接的な情報（不審な行動、会話の断片）
  - Criminal's evidence: looks irrelevant but becomes important later
- **initial_alibis** (3 cards):
  - Card 1: 自分のアリバイ（partner + room_id）
  - Card 2: 他の誰かの行動目撃
  - Card 3: 別の誰かの行動・発言情報
  - Alibi consistency: if A's partner is B, B's partner must be A with matching room_id
  - Criminal's alibi is false

## Constraints

- Exactly one criminal among player characters
- Each player has unique secret and objective
- Mystery must be solvable through clues
- Do NOT include "phases" field
- All text in Japanese
- Map supports crime logic (alibis, escape routes, evidence placement)
