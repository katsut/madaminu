## Map Structure

The map is a **floor plan** rendered as an SVG. You design the layout by placing rooms on a grid.

### Areas
- Each area is a floor or zone: a floor of a building, outdoor area, separate building, etc.
- area_type: "indoor" (建物内), "outdoor" (屋外), or "semi_outdoor" (テラス等)
- Each area has 3-8 rooms (including corridors, stairs, entrances)
- Examples:
  - 洋館: 本館1階, 本館2階, 中庭, 離れ
  - リゾートホテル: ロビー階, 客室階, プールサイド
  - 豪華客船: デッキ, 客室エリア, 機関室, レストラン階
  - 学校: 校舎1階, 校舎2階, 校庭, 体育館

### Rooms and room_type
Each room has: id, name, room_type, row, col, features.

**room_type** determines how it appears on the floor plan:
- `"room"` — 普通の部屋 (寝室, 台所, 書斎, etc.)
- `"corridor"` — 廊下。横長に表示される。部屋はドアで廊下につながる
- `"entrance"` — 玄関/出入口。屋内と屋外を結ぶ。特別なアイコン付き
- `"stairs"` — 階段室。別フロア(エリア)への接続点。段々模様で表示

**size** — 部屋の広さ。1=小部屋, 2=中部屋, 3=大部屋。広い部屋ほど調査ポイント(features)を多くする:
- size 1: features 3個（小部屋: クローゼット、物置、トイレなど）
- size 2: features 6個（中部屋: 書斎、寝室、キッチンなど）
- size 3: features 9個（大部屋: リビング、ホール、庭園など）
- 廊下・階段・入口: features 1-2個
廊下・階段・玄関には size 不要。

**row, col** — グリッド座標 (0始まり)。実際の間取りに合わせて配置する。

#### Indoor area layout example (洋館1階):
```
row 0: [玄関ホール]  [廊下(横長)]  [階段]
row 1: [リビング]    [ダイニング]  [台所]
row 2: [書斎]
```
- 廊下は row 0, col 1 に配置。部屋は廊下の下(row 1)に並ぶ
- 玄関は廊下の左端(row 0, col 0)、階段は右端(row 0, col 2)
- 部屋と廊下は "door" で接続、廊下同士は "corridor" で接続

#### Outdoor area layout example (庭園):
```
row 0: [前庭]  [温室]  [東屋]
```
- 屋外は廊下不要。場所を横一列 or 自由に配置
- 場所同士は "corridor"(小道) で接続

### CRITICAL layout rules
1. **廊下を必ず含めること (indoor areas)**。実際の建物には廊下がある。部屋は全て廊下にドアで接続する。廊下セルが足りない場合は複数の廊下セル（同じ名前でOK）を隣接配置して1本の長い廊下を表現する
2. **玄関を含めること**。屋内エリアから屋外エリアへの出口。玄関を経由しないと外に出られない
3. **階段を含めること**。1階→2階など別フロアへの移動は階段室を経由する
4. **階段はバックボーンの末尾に配置**。入口→廊下→…→階段の順。1階と2階で階段の位置（順番）を揃えること
5. **階段は廊下からのみ接続**。階段と部屋を直接つなげない。必ず廊下→階段の形にする
6. **部屋は廊下からのみ接続**。部屋同士を直接つなげない（hidden_passageを除く）。必ず廊下→部屋の形にする
7. **1つの廊下ノードに接続する部屋は最大2つ**。3つ以上の部屋を1つの廊下に繋げない。部屋が多い場合は廊下ノードを追加する
8. **隣接するセルの部屋のみ接続可能**。row/colが上下左右に隣接するセル同士だけconnectionを張れる
9. **接続の名前は双方向で一致させる**。A→Bの接続を定義したら、B→Aは定義しない（1つのconnectionで双方向）
10. **間取りはリアルに**。洋館なら洋館の、客船なら客船の間取りを想像して配置する

### Connections (edges)
- Each connection links two **grid-adjacent** rooms
- type: "door" (部屋↔廊下), "corridor" (廊下同士, 屋外の小道), "stairs" (階段↔別エリアの階段), "window", "hidden_passage"
- A connection can only exist between rooms whose (row,col) differ by exactly 1 in one axis
- Exception: "stairs" type connects rooms in DIFFERENT areas (same position rule does not apply)
- Exception: "hidden_passage" may connect non-adjacent rooms

### Route constraints (important for alibis and mystery logic)
- Think about how people move through the building
- "廊下からしか裏庭に出られない" → the corridor is a bottleneck
- "階段からしか2階に上がれない" → the staircase is the only link between floors
- Corridors and entrances are hub rooms with many connections
- Dead-end rooms (storage, secret room) have only one connection
- This topology creates natural alibis

### IMPORTANT: Generate an original map
- DO NOT copy the examples. Design a unique floor plan that fits your scenario's setting.
- Every scenario should produce a different map layout.

### Guidelines
- Generate 3-5 areas with a total of 15-20 rooms
- このうち調査可能な部屋（room_type="room"）は最低8個以上。廊下・階段・入口だけの階はNG
- 各エリアに最低2つの調査可能な部屋（room_type="room"）を含める
- 例: 本館1階なら「リビング」「書斎」「キッチン」「ダイニング」+ 廊下・玄関
- Include at least one lockable or restricted room
- The crime scene should have at least 2 connections
- Room IDs from the map are used for investigation locations during gameplay

## Character Profile Enhancement

Before generating the scenario, review each player's profile. If any field is too short, vague, or implausible for a murder mystery setting, enhance it:

- If a character is a non-human (animal, robot, etc.), add a plausible reason they can communicate (e.g., "人語を解する猫", "高度なAI搭載のロボット")
- If personality is only a few words, expand it to 2-3 sentences
- If background is too brief, add context that ties into the mystery setting
- If appearance is empty, generate an appropriate appearance based on gender, age, and occupation
- If occupation is empty, infer one from the background

### CRITICAL: Profile Consistency Check

シナリオ内の全テキスト（secret_info, objective, public_info, relationships）は、各キャラクターの性別・年齢・職業・経歴と矛盾しないこと:

- 50代男性を「一人娘」「令嬢」と呼ばない
- 20代のキャラクターに「30年前の事件」を経験させない
- 性別に合った呼称を使う（息子/娘、兄/姉、夫/妻など）
- 職業と行動の整合性（医者が法律の専門知識を持つなら理由が必要）
- キャラクター間の関係性が年齢的に成立するか確認する

Include these enhanced profiles in the player data output (as part of gm_notes).

## Secrets, Objectives, and Relationships

- Each player's secret_info and objective MUST involve relationships with other specific players
- Examples of relationship-driven elements:
  - "You must protect Player X because they know your alibi"
  - "You need to frame Player Y to divert suspicion from yourself"
  - "Player Z witnessed something that could expose your secret"
  - "You owe a debt to Player W and cannot betray them"
- Each player should have at least one ally and one adversary among the other players
- The criminal's objective should include hiding their crime while achieving a personal goal
- Non-criminal players should have objectives that may conflict with finding the truth
- public_info is NOT a repeat of the character profile. It is situation-specific information that all players can see:
  - Why this character came to this gathering / event
  - Who they arrived with or who invited them
  - Their publicly known relationship to the victim or other characters
  - What they were seen doing before the incident
  - Their stated reason for being here
  - Example: "被害者の古い友人として招待された。田中健二と一緒に来館し、到着時にロビーで他の客と談笑していた。夕食前に被害者と二人で書斎に入るのが目撃されている。"
  - Each public_info should mention at least one other character by name

## Initial Evidence & Alibis

Each player receives exactly 1 evidence card and 1 alibi card at the start:

- **initial_evidence**: 個人的に目撃・発見した情報。犯人のものは犯行と無関係に見えるがあとで重要になる曖昧な証拠
- **initial_alibi**: 事件発生時に他のキャラクターと一緒にいたことの証言
  - **partner** フィールドに相手のキャラクター名を記載
  - **アリバイの整合性**: AのアリバイでpartnerがBなら、Bのアリバイでもpartnerが必ずAで、同じ内容であること
  - 犯人のアリバイは偽り（実際は犯行時間に不在だった）
- **重複禁止**: initial_evidence と initial_alibi の内容は public_info と重複しないこと。public_info は公開情報、evidence/alibi は非公開の個人情報

## Constraints

- Exactly one player must be the criminal
- The criminal must be one of the player characters
- Each player must have a unique secret and objective
- The mystery must be solvable through the clues provided
- Do NOT include a "phases" field — phases are managed by the server
- All text content must be in Japanese
- The map layout must support the crime's logic (alibis, escape routes, evidence placement)
- Alibi pairs MUST be consistent: if A's alibi partner is B, then B's alibi partner MUST be A with matching content
